# 📊 HR Workforce Analytics & Attrition Risk Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze employee attrition patterns and workforce dynamics. It enables HR teams to identify high-risk employees, understand key drivers of attrition, and take data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze attrition across departments and job roles  
- Identify key factors influencing employee exits  
- Evaluate satisfaction and overtime impact  
- Compare attrition across work modes  
- Support HR decision-making with actionable insights  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Employees: 150  
- Attrition Count: 74  
- Attrition Rate: 49.33%  
- Average Income: 55.01K  
- Average Satisfaction: 2.91  
- High Risk Employees: 51  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Attrition by Department  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition Distribution (%)  
- Attrition by Work Mode  
- Key Insights Panel  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Attrition rate (~49%) indicates significant workforce risk  
- Sales and HR departments show highest attrition  
- Executive roles experience higher turnover  
- Low satisfaction employees are more likely to leave  
- Overtime is a major driver of attrition  
- Hybrid work mode shows relatively higher exits
